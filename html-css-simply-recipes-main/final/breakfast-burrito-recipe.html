Breakfast Burrito

Breakfast burrito is a popular breakfast dish that originated in the southwestern United States, specifically in regions with a strong Mexican-American influence. It is a hearty and filling meal that consists of a tortilla wrap filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, and a variety of other ingredients such as sausage, bacon, beans, potatoes, and vegetables like peppers and onions. The breakfast burrito became popular in the 1970s and 80s, as a convenient and portable breakfast option for busy Americans. It quickly gained popularity and spread across the country, with many restaurants and fast-food chains adding it to their menus.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper.

step 2

In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Add the onion and bell pepper and saut√© for 2-3 minutes, until softened.

step 3

Add the diced ham or cooked bacon to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until heated through.

step 4

Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, for 4-5 minutes, until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.

step 5

Warm the flour tortillas in the microwave or on a griddle. Spoon the egg mixture onto each tortilla, top with shredded cheddar cheese, and roll up into a burrito.

step 6

Serve the breakfast burritos hot, with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole if desired.

ingredients

8 large eggs

1/4 cup milk

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1 tablespoon butter

1/2 cup chopped onion

1/2 cup chopped bell pepper

1/2 cup diced ham or cooked bacon

4 large flour tortillas

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

tools

Medium bowl

Whisk

Large stillet

Wooden speen or spatula

Microwave or griddle (optional)
