Oats-Porridge

Oats porridge is a classic breakfast dish that has been enjoyed for centuries in many parts of the world. It is a simple and nutritious meal made from rolled oats, water or milk, and a pinch of salt, and is often served with sweeteners such as honey or maple syrup, as well as fruits and nuts for added flavor and texture. Oats are a whole-grain food that are rich in fiber, protein, and a variety of vitamins and minerals, making them an excellent choice for a healthy breakfast. They are also naturally gluten-free and can be enjoyed by people with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

prep time

2 min.

cook time

5-7 mins.

serving

1-2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

In a small saucepan, combine the rolled oats, water or milk, and a pinch of salt. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.

step 2

Once the mixture starts boiling, reduce the heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats are cooked and the mixture has thickened to your liking.

step 3

If desired, add sweeteners such as honey or maple syrup to taste, and stir well.

step 4

Serve the oats porridge hot in bowls. Top with sliced fruits or nuts if desired.

step 5

Enjoy your delicious and nutritious oats porridge! Note: You can adjust the amount of liquid used to your desired consistency - more liquid will result in a thinner porridge, while less liquid will be thicker.

ingredients

1/2 cup rolled oats

1 cup water or milk

Pinch of salt

Optional:seeteners(honey, maple syrup), fruits (banana,berries), nuts (almonds, walnuts)

tools

Small saucepan

Spoon for stirring
