Fruit Salad

Fruit salad is a popular dish made from various types of fresh fruits that are typically cut into small pieces and mixed together in a bowl. It is a healthy and refreshing dessert or snack that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. The origins of fruit salad are not clear, but it is believed to have been a popular dish in ancient times, as many cultures around the world have their own versions of it. Today, fruit salad is enjoyed in many different ways, with a variety of fruits and sometimes even other ingredients such as yogurt or honey added to enhance its flavor.

prep time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Wash and dry the fruits, and chop them if necessary. Place the mixed fruits in a large mixing bowl.

step 2

In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the honey and lime juice until well combined.

step 3

Pour the honey-lime mixture over the fruits in the large mixing bowl, and gently toss to coat the fruits with the mixture.

step 4

Add the fresh mint leaves to the bowl, and gently toss again to combine.
