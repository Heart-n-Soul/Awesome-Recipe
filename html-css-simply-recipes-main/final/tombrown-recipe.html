Tom Brown

Tom Brown is a meal that is made with roasted maize, groundnuts, soya beans. Afterwards, these foods are grinded into a uniform powdered flour. The flour is poured into water and boiled for some time and mixed together into a uniform paste. This meal is mostly taken as breakfast but can also be taken as lunch or desert.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2-4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Pour four cups into a nonstick bowl and put over medium heat. Crack two eggs into a small mixing bowl and season with salt and pepper. Beat the eggs with a fork or whisk until the yolks and whites are fully combined.

step 2

Optional: Add a little salt to the water. Pour the two cups of flour into the water after two to three minutes and stir continuously.

step 3

Stir continuously to prevent lumps from forming. When the mixture becomes a thick, you can serve or add a little water and stir to make it a little softer. Serve with sugar or honey, milk, and or bread.

ingredients

2 cups of Tombrown flour

4 cups of water

Optional: sugar , milk, powdered milk, sliced bread.
