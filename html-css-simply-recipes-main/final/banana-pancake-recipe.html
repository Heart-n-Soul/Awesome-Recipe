Banana Pancake

Banana pancakes are a type of pancake made by adding mashed bananas to the batter. They are a popular breakfast food and a great way to use up ripe bananas. Banana pancakes can be made with a variety of flours, including all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, and even almond flour for a gluten-free option. They can also be made with different types of milk, such as cow's milk, almond milk, or coconut milk. The addition of bananas not only adds flavor and natural sweetness but also adds moisture and texture to the pancakes. Banana pancakes are often served with syrup, whipped cream, or additional banana slices on top.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

In a medium bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until they are mostly smooth.

step 2

Beat the eggs into the mashed bananas until well combined.

step 3

In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture and stir until just combined.

step 4

Stir in the milk until the batter is smooth.

step 5

Heat a nonstick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat. Add the oil and swirl to coat the bottom of the pan.

step 6

Use a 1/4 cup measuring cup to scoop the batter onto the hot skillet. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

step 7

Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, honey, sliced bananas or fresh berries.

ingredients

2 ripe bananas

2 eggs

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 cup milk

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

tools

Medium bowl

Fork

Whisk

Measuring cups and spoons

Nonstick skillet or griddle

Spatula
