Pizza

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy but has now become popular all over the world. Its roots can be traced back to ancient civilizations in the Mediterranean, where flatbreads were topped with various ingredients and baked in stone ovens. However, the modern pizza as we know it today was first made in Naples, Italy, in the late 18th century. The original Neapolitan pizza was made with simple, fresh ingredients like tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil, and was cooked in a wood-fired oven. It became popular among the working class in Naples, and soon spread throughout Italy and beyond. Today, pizza is a global phenomenon and has been adapted to suit different cultural tastes, with toppings ranging from classic margherita to more experimental options like barbecue chicken or pineapple.

prep time

15-20 min.

cook time

12-15 min.

serving

4-6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

step 2

Place the rolled-out pizza dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch of crust around the edge.

step 3

Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the sauce. Add your desired toppings on top of the cheese.

step 4

Drizzle olive oil over the toppings and sprinkle dried oregano on top.

step 5

Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

ingredients

1 pound pizza dough

1/2 cup tomato sauce

2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1tablespoon dried oregano

Optional toppings:sliced pepperoni, slices mushrooms, slices onions, diced bell peppers, sliced clack olives.

tools

Rolling pin

Pizza pan or baking sheet

Spoon or spatula for spreading sauce

Grater for Parmesan cheese

Oven mitts

Knife for slicing
