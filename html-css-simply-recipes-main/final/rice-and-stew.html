Rice and Tomato Stew

Rice and tomato stew is a popular dish in many cultures around the world, particularly in Africa and the Caribbean. It is a simple yet flavorful dish that typically consists of rice, tomatoes, and various spices and seasonings. Depending on the region, additional ingredients such as vegetables, meats, or seafood may be added to the stew. The dish is often enjoyed as a hearty and filling meal on its own, or as a side dish to complement other main courses. It is also a popular vegetarian and vegan option due to its use of plant-based ingredients.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

35 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Rinse the rice with cold water and drain it.

step 2

In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent, about 3-4 minutes.

step 3

Add the can of crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine everything together.

step 4

Add the rice and water to the pot and stir well to combine. Bring the mixture to a boil.

step 5

Once it boils, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a lid, and simmer for 30-35 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.Use the spatula to fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape. Cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute, until the cheese is melted and the omelette is fully cooked.

ingredients

2 cup of rice

1 onion, chopped

3 cloves of garlic, minced

2 tablespoons of olive oil

1 can of crushed tomatoes (14.5 ounces)

1 teaspoon of dried basil

1 teaspoon of dried oregano

Salt and pepper to taste

4 cups of water

tools

Large pot with lid

Wooden spoon
