Vegetable Lasagna

Vegetable lasagna is a popular Italian dish that is a vegetarian version of traditional meat lasagna. It is made with layers of pasta, vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce. The dish originated in Italy, where it was typically made with spinach and ricotta cheese. Today, there are many variations of vegetable lasagna, with different types of vegetables and cheeses used. It has become a popular dish in many countries and is often served as a vegetarian option in restaurants.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

60 min.

serving

8-10 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

step 2

Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

step 3

In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well and set aside.

step 4

In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the bell pepper, squash, zucchini, onion, and garlic. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

step 5

Pour half of the pasta sauce into the skillet with the vegetables and stir to combine.

step 6

In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the vegetable and sauce mixture. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat with another layer of sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese mixture. Finish with a layer of sauce on top.

step 7

Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese on top of the lasagna.

step 8

Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

step 9

Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

ingredients

12 lasagna noodles

1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

1egg

1 teaspoon dried basil

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon black pepper

1 red bell pepper, diced

1 yellow squash, sliced

1 zuchini, sliced

1onion, diced

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 (24 oz) jar of your favorite pasta sauce

tools

large skillet

medium bowl

9x13 inch baking dish

foil
