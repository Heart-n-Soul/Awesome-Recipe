Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a dessert that is beloved all over the world. The origins of chocolate cake can be traced back to the mid-18th century, when chocolate was first introduced to Europe. Chocolate cake as we know it today, with its moist, fluffy texture and rich chocolate flavor, was perfected in the early 20th century with the development of new baking techniques and the use of high-quality ingredients like cocoa powder and chocolate chips. Today, chocolate cake is a staple at birthdays, weddings, and other celebrations, and there are countless variations on the classic recipe to suit every taste.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30-35 min.

serving

12-16 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round cake pans or one 13x9 inch baking pan.

step 2

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

step 3

Add in the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.

step 4

Stir in the boiling water, a little at a time, until the batter is smooth.

step 5

Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

step 6

Let the cake cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

2 cups granulated sugar

3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda

1 teaspoon salt

1 cup milk

1/2 cup vegetable oil

2 large eggs

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1 cup boiling water

tools

Two 9-inch round cake pans or one 13x9 inch baking pan

Large mixing bowl

Whisk

Measuring cups and spoons

Oven

Toothpick

Wire rack
